# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in any ecotel-it
repository, please report it privately. Do **not** open a public GitHub issue,
post in a public channel, or share details outside the trusted reporting path.

**Email:** security@ecotel.it

Please include, where possible:

- A clear description of the issue and the impact.
- Steps to reproduce, or a proof-of-concept.
- The repository, version, or commit hash affected.
- Your name and contact details for follow-up (we will credit you in the
  advisory unless you ask us not to).

## What to expect

- **Acknowledgement** within 2 working days.
- **Initial assessment** within 5 working days.
- **Coordinated disclosure**: we agree a timeline with you before any public
  advisory or fix release.

## Scope

This policy applies to all repositories under the `ecotel-it` GitHub
organization, including production code, internal tooling, and templates.

Out of scope:

- Vulnerabilities in third-party dependencies that we cannot fix without an
  upstream patch (we will, however, track them and apply mitigations).
- Social engineering of ecotel employees.
- Physical security of ecotel offices.

Thank you for helping us keep ecotel and its customers secure.
